## 📊 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer financial data to uncover key drivers of credit scores and assess overall credit risk.

Through comprehensive **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** and visualizations, the study evaluates the impact of variables such as income, outstanding debt, credit utilization, payment behavior, and loan history on customer creditworthiness.

The project also identifies anomalies and risky financial patterns that may signal potential defaults or fraudulent behavior.

### 🔍 Key Outcomes

* Identified major factors influencing credit score variations
* Detected high-risk customer segments based on financial behavior
* Highlighted relationships between debt, EMI burden, and creditworthiness

### 🎯 Business Impact

The insights can support financial institutions in:

* Improving **risk modeling and credit scoring systems**
* Enhancing **customer segmentation strategies**
* Enabling **smarter and more reliable lending decisions**
